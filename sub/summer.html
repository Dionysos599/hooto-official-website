N.E.X.T工作坊 N.E.X.T Workshop

为期七天的为12-15岁青少年设计的跨学科综合能力提升课程，涵盖艺术、工程、经济等领域，通过创新的认知训练与实践创作，帮助学生拓展思维边界。帮助他们拥有广阔的视野和多元的能力。 A seven-day cross-disciplinary comprehensive ability enhancement course designed for teenagers aged 12-15, covering art, engineering, economics and other fields. Through innovative cognitive training and practical creation, it helps students expand their thinking boundaries and develop broad perspectives and diverse abilities.

跨学科课程体验 Cross-disciplinary Course Experience

在四天的课程里，孩子们在课堂上学习着创造着，全都成为了小小艺术家。我们也看到了他们眼中闪耀的好奇，手中绽放的创意，和脸上纯真的笑容。孩子们也通过艺术去感知了自己和身边的世界。 During the four days of courses, children learned and created in the classroom, all becoming little artists. We also saw the curiosity shining in their eyes, the creativity blooming in their hands, and the pure smiles on their faces. Children also perceived themselves and the world around them through art.

我：展 Me: Exhibition

导师们带领小学员们在两天的努力下完成了展览的策划和搭建。所有的作品都是学员们在四天的课程中所创作的。经过大家的努力打磨，艺术展成功开幕。这既是艺术成果的展示，更是成长足迹的见证。 Under the guidance of mentors, young students completed the planning and construction of the exhibition through two days of hard work. All the works were created by students during the four-day course. Through everyone's efforts and refinement, the art exhibition successfully opened. This is not only a display of artistic achievements, but also a witness to the footprints of growth.

展览现场 Exhibition Scene

经过精心策划和布置，展览现场展现了学员们丰富的创意和独特的艺术视角。每一件作品都承载着孩子们的梦想和努力。 After careful planning and arrangement, the exhibition scene showcases the rich creativity and unique artistic perspectives of the students. Each work carries the dreams and efforts of the children.
