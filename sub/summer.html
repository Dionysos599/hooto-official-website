N.E.X.T WORKSHOP

为12–15岁青少年设计的跨学科综合能力提升课程An interdisciplinary course designed for teenagers aged 12–15

(01) / 04
N.E.X.T工作坊N.E.X.T Workshop
AGE12–15
DURATION7 Days
FIELDSArt / Engineering / Economics

从好奇出发，
向世界提问。Start curious.
Question the world.

为期七天的为12-15岁青少年设计的跨学科综合能力提升课程，涵盖艺术、工程、经济等领域，通过创新的认知训练与实践创作，帮助学生拓展思维边界。帮助他们拥有广阔的视野和多元的能力。

A seven-day cross-disciplinary comprehensive ability enhancement course designed for teenagers aged 12-15, covering art, engineering, economics and other fields. Through innovative cognitive training and practical creation, it helps students expand their thinking boundaries and develop broad perspectives and diverse abilities.

(02)跨学科课程体验Course Experience

学习，也在
创造。Learning
through making.

在四天的课程里，孩子们在课堂上学习着创造着，全都成为了小小艺术家。我们也看到了他们眼中闪耀的好奇，手中绽放的创意，和脸上纯真的笑容。孩子们也通过艺术去感知了自己和身边的世界。During four days of courses, children learned and created in the classroom, becoming little artists who perceived themselves and the world around them through art.

(03) / 04
我：展Me: Exhibition

作品完成后，
展览才开始。After making,
we exhibit.

导师们带领小学员们在两天的努力下完成了展览的策划和搭建。所有的作品都是学员们在四天的课程中所创作的。经过大家的努力打磨，艺术展成功开幕。这既是艺术成果的展示，更是成长足迹的见证。

Under the guidance of mentors, young students completed the planning and construction of the exhibition through two days of hard work. This is not only a display of artistic achievements, but also a witness to the footprints of growth.

(04)开营现场Opening Scene

成长足迹，
被看见。Growth,
made visible.

开营现场记录了学员与导师共同开启跨学科旅程的瞬间。Opening moments of the interdisciplinary workshop with mentors and students.
