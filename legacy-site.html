品牌Brand

Hooto于2024年落地生根，名称来源于初创团队的家乡方言——吴语——“花头精”一词的音译。旨在从不同艺术角度出发，通过将姑苏文化与国际视野结合的方式解读“花花世界”，将主创团队从少年时期便建立的深厚羁绊，化作脚踏实地的动力。从设计青少年艺术文化教育、打造特色文创产品等形式，为创作注入多元色彩与文化温度，助推下一代的文化认同与自我探索。 Hooto took root in 2024. Its name derives from the transliteration of the Wu dialect term "花头精" from the founding team's hometown. We aim to interpret the "colorful world" from diverse artistic perspectives by combining Suzhou culture with an international outlook, transforming the profound bond formed in our youth into tangible motivation. Through designing youth-focused art and cultural education programs and creating distinctive cultural and creative products, we inject diverse color and cultural warmth into our creations, fostering the next generation's cultural identity and self-discovery.

朋辈教育Peer Education

团队导师涵盖体制内外多样背景，善于用真实经历共情青少年心理。在课程中，我们更像"过来人"而非"说教者"，有效化解师生之间的代际距离，让学生更自然地接受思考引导与价值共鸣。 Our mentors come from diverse backgrounds inside and outside institutional settings, adept at empathizing with youth psychology through real-life experiences. In our courses, we act more as "those who have been through it" rather than "preachers," effectively bridging the generational gap between teachers and students, allowing learners to naturally embrace guided thinking and shared values.

国际视野 & 专业性Global Vision & Professionalism

团队成员来自全球多所顶尖综合类高校和艺术院校，涵盖插画、平面设计、建筑、工程等多个领域。我们具备扎实的专业基础与审美执行力，能够满足绝大部分市场需求的艺术形式。 Our team members hail from top comprehensive and art institutions worldwide, covering fields such as illustration, graphic design, architecture, and engineering. We boast a solid professional foundation and aesthetic execution capability, capable of meeting most market demands for artistic forms.
