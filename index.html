(01) 我们相信Our belief

先有好奇，
再有答案。 Curiosity
before answers.

Hooto于2024年落地生根，名称来源于初创团队的家乡方言——吴语——“花头精”一词的音译。旨在从不同艺术角度出发，通过将姑苏文化与国际视野结合的方式解读“花花世界”，助推下一代的文化认同与自我探索。

Hooto took root in 2024. Its name derives from the transliteration of the Wu dialect term “花头精” from the founding team's hometown. We interpret the colorful world by combining Suzhou culture with an international outlook, fostering cultural identity and self-discovery.
